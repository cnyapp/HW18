Hi there, I'm Courtney

I’m Courtney Yapp, but my friends call me Coco. I’m a UX Designer with a background in Industrial Design. In my design work, I strives to create meaningful solutions to help people connect to the physical and digital world around them. As a curious and inquisitive person, I love researching to better understand the full scope of a project, from our users needs, history of the project, and other valuable insights along the way that help drive toward an insightful solution. With my diverse work experience from other design fields, I bring a diverse toolkit of skills to the design process to help a project come to life. When I am not designing, you can find me at my local climbing gym, on a hike with my dog Otto, drawing, or trying to prove I have a green thumb.
